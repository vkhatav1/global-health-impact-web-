WHAT DO WE DO?


Imagine a world where people everywhere have access to the life-saving drugs they need to fight diseases like tuberculosis, malaria, and HIV/AIDS. The Global Health Impact Index – supported by a collaboration of researchers from universities and civil society organizations around the world – opens the door to positive change by measuring pharmaceutical products’ impact on global health.
